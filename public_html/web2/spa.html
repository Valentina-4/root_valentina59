My Top Artists

Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham also known as “Drake” is a very popular Canadian rap musician and one of the world's best-selling music artists. His top song being “One Dance” which was released in 2016 and currently stands at over 3.5 billion streams. Drake is one of my favorite artists because of his relatable lyrics ,collaborations with other popular artists, and variety of genres.His versatility and lyrics make him one of my favorites. With countless chart-topping songs and record-breaking achievements, Drake has cemented his place as one of the greatest musicians of his generation.

Kid Cudi

Scott Ramon Seguro Mescudi also known as “Kid CuDi” is an American born rapper, singer, producer, and songwriter. He is known for his creative and unique style in hip-hop, and addresses life struggles in his work like mental health and childhood experiences.Cudi's most popular song is “Memories” with over 1 billion streams released in 2010. Since hearing that song, I fell in love with his dream-like melodies and needed to hear more!

Men I Trust

“Men I Trust” is an indie band that originates from Quebec City. In 2014, three high school friends, Emmanuelle Proulx, Jessy Varon, and Dragos Chiriac, came together to create their most popular song, “Show Me How,” in 2018, which has almost 6 million streams. Known for their laid-back style and mellow instrumentation, the band has garnered a devoted following. Their smooth, ethereal sound creates an atmosphere that's hard to resist, which is why they are one of my favorite artists.
